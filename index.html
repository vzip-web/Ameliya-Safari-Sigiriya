Our Story

About Ameliya

Born from a deep love for Sri Lanka's wild spaces, Ameliya Elephant Safari has been the trusted gateway to Sigiriya's untamed beauty for over a decade. We are not just a tour company — we are storytellers of the jungle, guardians of the wild, and your companions on an unforgettable adventure.

Our expert local guides have grown up in the shadow of Sigiriya Rock, understanding every trail, every season, and every secret the jungle holds. From the awe-inspiring Elephant Gathering at Minneriya to the soul-stirring sunrise atop Pidurangala, we craft experiences that stay with you forever.

Every safari with us is a promise — a promise of authentic encounters, responsible tourism, and memories that echo through a lifetime.
